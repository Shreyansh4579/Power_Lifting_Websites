Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?

+

Most athletes see strength improvements within 2-4 weeks. Significant changes in technique and programming efficiency typically occur within the first training block (4-8 weeks).

What if I have limited equipment?

+

We work with whatever equipment you have access to. Whether it's a full commercial gym, home gym, or minimal equipment, we'll create effective programming for your situation.

Do you work with beginners?

+

Absolutely! We love working with beginners. We'll focus on building proper movement patterns, establishing good habits, and creating a solid foundation for long-term progress.

How often do we communicate?

+

Communication frequency depends on your plan. Starter plans include monthly check-ins, Plus plans include weekly communication, and Elite plans include daily support and 24/7 access.

What if I need to pause my coaching?

+

We understand life happens! You can pause your coaching at any time with 30 days notice. We'll provide you with a maintenance program to keep you on track until you're ready to resume.
