Daily Targets (rule of thumb)

  • Protein: 1.6–2.2 g per kg bodyweight
  • Fat: 0.6–1.0 g per kg bodyweight
  • Carbs: fill remaining calories for energy

Timing Tips

  • Pre-workout: 25–60 g carbs + 20–40 g protein
  • Post-workout: 20–40 g protein within 2 hours
  • Hydration: bodyweight (kg) × 30–40 ml per day
